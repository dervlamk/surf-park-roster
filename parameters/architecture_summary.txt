STAFFING MODEL DESIGN
---------------------

Person model (solver/staff.py)
  Key flags:
    reef_eligible    — surf certification; can work reef stations (RG/RF)
    coach_eligible   — WSI certification; can teach lessons (AC/BC slots)
  A person with BOTH flags is the "dual-role" employee: covers reef LG
  duties AND advanced coaching slots without an extra hire.

  Contract types: FT | PT | CASUAL
    PT:     < 40 h/week (preferred per hiring_guidelines.txt §1)
    FT:     triggered after 90 consecutive days at 30+ h/week (CA law §8)
    CASUAL: on-call, no minimum

Shift structure (per lifeguard_rules.txt §12-13)
  Base shift:    4.5 h (9 × 30-min slots)
  Extended:      up to 8.5 h (17 slots) for longer days
  Rule §13:      extend mid-shifts BEFORE extending opening/closing shifts
  CA meal break: any shift > 5 h gets one 30-min unpaid lunch
  CA rest break: one 10-min paid break per 3.5 h worked

Shift-cover CP-SAT model (solver/staffing.py)
  For each role type (shore, reef, adv_coach, bay_coach):
    - Compute demand[t] = concurrent workers of this type needed at slot t
    - Generate feasible shift windows (start, end) aligned to hour boundaries
    - Decision: n_workers[window] ∈ {0..10} integer
    - Constraint: for each t, sum(n_workers[w] covering t) >= demand[t]
    - Objective: minimize sum(n_workers) across all windows and types

  Dual-role optimization:
    Reef workers with coach_eligible can satisfy both reef AND adv_coach demand
    simultaneously — no separate hire needed for progressive wave coaching.
    Model via separate variables: reef_dual[w] (reef_eligible + coach_eligible)
    and reef_only[w]. Constraints:
      reef_dual + reef_only >= reef_demand[t]
      reef_dual             >= adv_coach_demand[t]

  Bay-coach-from-LG optimization:
    Bay coach slots (BC*) require WSI cert but NOT surf cert.
    LGs (shore or reef) who hold WSI can fill BC slots during lesson windows.
    On long operating days, a single LG with WSI who spends 2.5 h on a
    bay lesson instead of an LG rotation slot can eliminate a separate
    coach hire. Model as a constraint-relaxation option flagged in output.

Weekly solve strategy (avoids the joint-model timeout)
  Solve each day independently (fast, 90-150 s each).
  Sum each person's daily hours across the week post-solve.
  Flag anyone crossing the 30 h/week FT trigger (CA law).
  No joint CP-SAT model needed — weekly hour checks are a post-step.

Headcount output (per day)
  For each role type and shift window, report:
    - n_workers: minimum concurrent workers in that window
    - shift_hours: length of window
    - ca_break_required: bool (shift > 5 h)
  Totals:
    - total_person_shifts: sum of all n_workers across windows
    - min_unique_persons (all PT, one shift each) = total_person_shifts
    - min_unique_persons (some FT, two adjacent shifts) < total_person_shifts
    - dual_role_savings: adv_coach slots coverable by existing reef guards
    - bay_coach_savings: BC slots coverable by LGs with WSI
