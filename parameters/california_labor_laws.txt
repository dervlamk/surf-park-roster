California Labor Law
 1. Any shift over 5 hours must have an unpaid 30 minute lunch break
 2. If a break is required, the break must start before the 5th hour of a shift
 3. For every 3.5 hours of work a 10 minute paid break must be given
 4. A break cannot be added onto a lunch break 
 5. A break cannot be used to go home early
 6. A 6 hour shift can have a meal waiver, but I want to avoid this strategy, but have it noted.
